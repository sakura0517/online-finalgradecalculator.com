How to Use a Points-Based Grade Calculator

A points-based grade calculator operates on the principle of total points earned divided by total points possible. Instead of dealing with weights, this method tallies all your scores and calculates a percentage. For example, if you've earned 450 points out of 500 possible, your current grade is 90%. This approach is straightforward and ideal for classes that use a cumulative points system.

Converting Points to Percentages

To convert points to percentages, divide the points you earned on an assignment by the total points available, then multiply by 100. For example, earning 18 out of 20 points results in a 90%. Many grade calculators, including ours, support both points-based and weighted systems, helping you track progress in whichever format your instructor uses.
